Privacy Policy — Bigger Than Bob

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bigger Than Bob ("the app") handles your information. The app is provided by Equus Media ("we", "us"). If you have any questions, contact us at privacy@eqpc.co.uk.

Who this policy covers

This policy applies to everyone who uses Bigger Than Bob, a personal strength-training tracker for Android. By creating an account and using the app, you agree to the handling of information described below.

What we collect

To provide the app's features, we collect and store the following:

  • Account details — the email address and password you use to sign in. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider and are stored in hashed form; we never see or store your password in plain text.
  • Training data — the workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weights and progression history you record in the app.
  • Body-weight entries — any body-weight values you choose to log.
  • Crew & sharing data — if you join or create a "crew", we store your chosen display name and the specific statistics you opt to share (for example personal records, training volume, session counts or streaks). You control which metrics are shared, and some (such as body weight and weight change) are off by default.
  • On-device data — a local copy of your app data and settings is stored on your device so the app works offline. Reminder notifications are scheduled locally on your device.

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics, and the app contains no third-party advertising.

How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and let you sign in across devices.
  • To save your training history and sync it so it is available when you reinstall or switch device.
  • To power the progression engine, progress charts and muscle-activity view.
  • To provide the optional crew and leaderboard features, showing only the statistics you have chosen to share with members of a crew you belong to.
  • To schedule the workout and rest reminders you enable.

Who your data is shared with

  • Other crew members. If you join a crew, the display name and metrics you have opted to share are visible to other members of that crew. Nothing is shared publicly, and nothing is shared if you do not join a crew.
  • Our hosting provider (Supabase). Your account and training data are stored on servers operated by Supabase, which acts as our data processor. Access is protected by row-level security so that each account can only access its own data (and shared crew data as described above).
  • We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers or data brokers.

Data storage and security

Data is stored on our provider's secured servers and transmitted over encrypted connections. Access is restricted by per-user security rules. While no online service can be guaranteed 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. You can request that your account and all associated data be permanently deleted by emailing privacy@eqpc.co.uk. We will action deletion requests within a reasonable period.

Your rights

Depending on your location (including under UK/EU data protection law), you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@eqpc.co.uk.
